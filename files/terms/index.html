About

This isn't a legal document or some copy-paste license. This is just me (Ronyx69) explaining how I think this content should be used.

Some of my work contains copyrighted content used without permission - e.g. the Honda logo on my Honda Stepwgn model. So I don't consider myself to be entitled to dictate how you are allowed to use this content in your work. You have to decide for yourself.

Context

This content was created for Cities: Skylines, and I expect that it will be mostly used in the same context by others. But if you want to convert these items for other games or create artwork using it, I don't mind at all, as long as it's done fairly according to these terms.

Branding & Affiliation

If you display my logo or use my "branding", you should not do so in a way that misleads people into thinking that I was personally involved in or approved of your projects.

Credit

If you use any of my work in a public manner, I expect to be credited and maybe even a link to the source files page or the particular item that you based your work on. If you don't credit me, I will be somewhat disappointed but I won't go hunting you down.

Profit

If you make small amounts of money (e.g. a 5$ donation a month) from your work which was based on my work, don't worry about it, I don't care. If you make a big amount of money (e.g. 100$ for a commission), then I should receive a cut of the profits proportional to my contribution. If my work has helped you in any way, you can donate privately or publicly, or buy the JP office tutorial files, any support is appreciated.

Inquiry

You don't have to contact me for permission to use any of these files.

Intent

This was a hobby for me, but I don't mind making money from it. I have built on the work of others, and others can build on the work I've done or learn by looking at the way I've done things, and the players/consumers can get more and better content. I hope that whoever uses these files does the same, for the same reasons.
